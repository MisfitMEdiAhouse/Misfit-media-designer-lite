Why Breezy Service

Utah’s home ventilation experts right in your backyard. We help homeowners reduce trapped heat, improve airflow, and create a cooler, more comfortable home with whole home fans and solar attic fans.

Breezy Attic Fan

Powered by the sun, our solar attic fan vents superheated attic air before it radiates into your living space.

  • Improves efficiency and roof longevity
  • Removes trapped attic heat at the source
  • Lowers ceiling temps and AC strain

Breezy Whole House Fan

Pull cool evening air through your home in minutes while flushing out stale, trapped indoor heat.

  • Draws in cool outdoor air naturally
  • Rapid cooling for living spaces and bedrooms
  • Uses far less energy than central A/C

The Full Breezy System

Pair attic ventilation with whole home airflow for a stronger cooling solution from the roof down into the living space.

  • Increases comfort throughout the home
  • Helps reduce A/C strain
  • Supports lower summer cooling costs
