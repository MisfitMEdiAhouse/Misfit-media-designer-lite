Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 28, 2026

Misfit AI is operated by JPrice Media LLC. This Privacy Policy explains how Misfit AI collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you interact with our website, AI demo, trial signup, onboarding flow, appointment scheduling, SMS workflows, or related services.

Information We Collect

We may collect information you provide directly to Misfit AI, including:

  • Name, email address, mobile phone number, and business name
  • Information submitted through the AI demo, signup flow, onboarding flow, booking flow, or contact request
  • SMS opt-in consent records, consent timestamps, source pages, and related communication preferences
  • Lead follow-up preferences, appointment details, service interests, and customer communication history
  • Technical information needed to operate, secure, and improve the Misfit AI platform

How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Respond to inquiries, demo requests, and onboarding requests
  • Create and support Misfit AI accounts and trial experiences
  • Provide AI lead follow-up, appointment booking, and customer communication workflows
  • Send service-related updates, reminders, and support communications
  • Maintain opt-in, opt-out, suppression, and consent records
  • Improve the reliability, safety, and performance of Misfit AI

SMS Communications

If you provide your mobile phone number and opt in, you agree to receive SMS messages from Misfit AI / JPrice Media LLC regarding your inquiry, demo request, onboarding, account setup, appointment scheduling, follow-up, support, and service-related updates.

Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out and HELP for help. Consent is not a condition of purchase.

SMS consent is collected only when a user actively provides their phone number and confirms opt-in through an approved Misfit AI signup, trial, demo, onboarding, appointment, or other direct opt-in flow.

Opt-Out and Help Instructions

You may opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP. After opting out, you may receive one final confirmation message, and future SMS messages will be suppressed unless you opt in again.

For help, reply HELP or contact us at info@misfitmediahouse.com.

Consent and Communication Records

Misfit AI may store consent records, opt-in timestamps, opt-out requests, message delivery records, and related compliance data to help document communication permissions and prevent messages from being sent to opted-out contacts.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your personal information with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

We may share information with trusted service providers that help us operate Misfit AI, including hosting, authentication, email, analytics, messaging, payment, and infrastructure providers. These providers are used only to support our services.

Mobile information and SMS consent are not shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

Customer Data

Misfit AI customers are responsible for using compliant contact data, honoring consent requirements, and following applicable texting rules when messaging their own leads and clients through the platform.

Data Retention

We retain information for as long as needed to provide services, maintain business records, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and support consent and suppression requirements.

Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to help protect information. No system can be guaranteed completely secure, but we work to protect data from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

Your Choices

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your information by contacting us. Some information may need to be retained for legal, operational, security, billing, or compliance purposes, including opt-out and consent records.
