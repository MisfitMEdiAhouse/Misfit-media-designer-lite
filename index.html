How Misfit AI messaging works

Misfit AI is designed for permission-based communication. SMS messages are intended for users who have opted in through an approved AI demo flow, trial signup, onboarding flow, inbound inquiry, appointment request, or existing business relationship.

Opt-in required

Misfit AI is not intended for scraped lists, purchased lists, or unsolicited bulk texting.

Opt-out supported

Recipients can reply STOP to opt out. Suppression logic helps prevent future messages to opted-out contacts.

Business-purpose messages

Messages are related to inquiries, follow-up, appointments, onboarding, account setup, reminders, and service updates.
